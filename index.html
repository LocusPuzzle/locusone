Locus | One

Locus is a series of puzzles spread out across the internet. You have found the first installment.

Those wishing to participate must contribute a minimum .1 Ether to stake the Locus contract, which will dispense 80% of each buy in to a bounty wallet for the puzzle winner and the remaining 20% into a dev wallet for use in developing future puzzles.

The first participant to successfully solve the puzzle will receive the full balance of the bounty address.

The first set of "Prologue" clues will be released as the bounty hits certain threshholds (1 ETH, 3 ETH, etc), and all remaining clues required to solve will be released by April 30th or once the bounty reaches 5 ETH in value, whichever comes first. At this point, the contract will be paused and therefore closed to new participants.

The winner will be required to verify full stake in the pot (at least .1 ETH) upon attempting to claim their reward.

The longer the puzzle takes to be solved, the more participants are likely to enter the game, and the larger the resulting bounty.
