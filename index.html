Locus | Chapter One

Welcome to Locus, a puzzle ecosystem spread out across the internet in several chapters, of which this is the first.

Those wishing to participate must contribute .05 Ether to the jackpot Ethereum address.

The first participant to successfully reach the end of the chapter will receive 80% of the jackpot accrued to that point, with the remaining 20% retained by Locus for use in developing further chapters of the puzzle, paying devs, etc.

The winner will be required to verify full stake in the pot before they will be eligible to receive their reward.

The longer the chapter takes to be solved, the more players are likely to enter the game, and the larger grows the bounty.
