Locus | One

Locus | One is an ARG riddle/scavenger hunt/logic puzzle spread out across the internet. You have found the first installment.

Locus takes players on a journey through the internet (and humanity)'s deep well of weirdness in order to uncover and progress a thematic narrative that passes through philosophy, literature, history, science, music, and much more. Clues could be anywhere at any time.

There are two ways to participate in Locus | One:
    1. Table Stakes: Solve the puzzle in its entirety for free, just for the fun of it.
    2. Bounty Race: Stake a bounty in Ether (ETH), and then compete to be the first to solve the puzzle and claim the bounty.

Rules for the Bounty Race

Those wishing to be eligible to claim the bounty must contribute a minimum 0.1 Ether to stake the Locus contract, which will dispense 80% of each buy-in to a bounty wallet for the puzzle winner and the remaining 20% into a dev wallet for use in developing future puzzles.

The first participant to successfully solve the puzzle will receive the full balance of the bounty address.

The winner will be required to verify full stake in the pot upon attempting to claim their reward.

Check the current balance of the pot here.

The first set of five "Prologue" levels are already live, and all remaining levels required to solve will be released on April 30th at 23:00 UTC.

Again, as this is about fun, you'll be able to access/solve the whole puzzle for free, but you'll only be able to claim the ETH bounty if you've staked in by 4/30. At this point, the contract will be paused and therefore closed to new participants in the bounty race.
